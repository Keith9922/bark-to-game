# Recipe: glitch_y2k

Late-90s web, datamoshing, chrome gradients. Pre-iPhone digital wonder.

## Palette
- background: chrome silver `#c0c0c0`
- primary: lime green `#00ff00`
- secondary: hot pink `#ff66cc`
- accent: electric blue `#1e90ff`
- glitch: cyan and red split channels

## Typography
- display: `"Impact", sans-serif` (oversized, condensed)
- body: `"Tahoma", "Verdana", sans-serif`

## Motion vocabulary
- 100–300 ms RGB channel separation on transitions
- Sudden scale snaps and slice glitches
- WordArt-style arc warps
- Animated rainbow gradients on text

## Asset cues
- Chrome bevel on UI panels (linear gradient highlight)
- Lens-flare star bursts on pickups
- Marquee scrolling tickers at top/bottom
- Spinning 3D-ish primitives (CSS transforms)

## Audio cues
- Dial-up modem screech for transitions
- 90s game show sting (rising synth horn)
- Crystalline arpeggio for success

## DO NOT
- Flat design, minimal palettes
- Restrained animation
- Modern sans-serifs (Inter, system-ui)
