# Recipe: isometric_pastel

Soft architectural toy world. Tilted-plane viewpoint, cosy palette.

## Palette
- background: butter cream `#fff4d9`
- primary: soft sage `#a8c8a0`
- secondary: muted rose `#e4a8a4`
- accent: warm terracotta `#cc7a52`
- shadow: dusty lavender `#b8a8c8`

## Typography
- display: `"Quicksand", sans-serif` (rounded, friendly)
- body: `"Nunito", sans-serif`

## Motion vocabulary
- Smooth ease-in-out (300–500 ms)
- Gentle bob/sway on stationary characters
- Soft drop-shadow during jumps (offset + blur)
- Spring-physics for character landing

## Asset cues
- Strict 2:1 isometric grid (30° axes)
- Rounded corners on every block (4–6 px radius)
- Soft cast shadows below every object
- Stack blocks like building toys; no harsh contrast

## Audio cues
- Marimba / kalimba / glockenspiel
- Soft pads with mild reverb
- Plinky pickup sounds, no harsh transients

## DO NOT
- Top-down or side-view perspective
- Saturated neon or pure black
- Sharp pixel edges
