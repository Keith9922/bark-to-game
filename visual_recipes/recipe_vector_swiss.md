# Recipe: vector_swiss

International Typographic Style applied to a tiny game. Restraint and rigour.

## Palette
- background: pure white `#ffffff`
- primary: pure black `#000000`
- accent: signal red `#e53935` (the ONLY chromatic element)
- midtone: cool grey `#b0b3b8`

## Typography
- display: `"Helvetica Neue", "Helvetica", sans-serif` (Bold)
- body: same family, Regular
- weight contrast carries hierarchy — never use italics or decoration

## Motion vocabulary
- Linear or single ease-out (150–200 ms)
- Single property change per transition — no compound animations
- Sharp on/off — no fades unless transitioning between scenes

## Asset cues
- Lines, circles, rectangles only — strictly geometric
- 8 px modular grid for alignment
- Hairline strokes (1 px) for divisions, 4–8 px for emphasis
- One accent colour, used surgically (≤5% of screen area)

## Audio cues
- Restraint over melody: short clicks, taps, single notes
- Pure sine waves; no harmony
- Silence is content; do not fill the room

## DO NOT
- Drop shadows, gradients, glows
- More than two type sizes per scene
- Any decorative element that does not serve hierarchy
